Privacy Policy

Your privacy is fully protected.

Queazy does not collect, store, or share any personal data. Everything stays on your device — always.

01 — Overview

About this Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to the Queazy mobile application (the "App"), developed by Viranaiken Jessy, based in France. It describes how your information is handled when you use the App.

By using Queazy, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this policy.

02 — Data Collection

We collect nothing.

Queazy does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. No account is required. No analytics, no crash reporting, no tracking of any kind.

All content you create — including flashcards and extracted text — is stored exclusively on your device and never leaves it.

03 — Permissions

Why we request access

Queazy requests the following device permissions solely to provide its core features. None of this data is ever transmitted outside your device.

📷
Camera

Used to photograph documents so Queazy can extract their text content. Photos are processed on-device only and are never saved or transmitted.

🖼️
Photo Library

Used to select existing images from your gallery for text extraction. Queazy only accesses images you explicitly choose.

📁
Files & Documents

Used to import documents (such as PDFs) from your device storage. Queazy only reads files you explicitly select.

04 — Data Storage

Everything stays on your device.

Flashcards and all generated content are stored locally on your iPhone using on-device storage. Queazy has no server, no cloud sync, and no backend. Deleting the app removes all associated data.

05 — Third Parties

No third-party services.

Queazy does not integrate any third-party SDKs for analytics, advertising, or data collection. No data is shared with any external party for any reason.

06 — Children

Safe for all ages.

Since Queazy collects no personal data, it is safe for users of all ages, including children under 13. There is nothing to collect, store, or share.

Policy updates

If this policy is ever updated, the new version will be published at this URL with a revised date at the top. Continued use of the App following any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
